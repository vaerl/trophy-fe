# Trophy

> Some general information for how to do to things the day of the trophy.

## Prerequisites

- having a walkie-talkie was useful, but we were too busy to answer most of the time

### Staff

Have at least three people, but no more than four.
The current (2024) setup was to have two people working the app (one for male/mixed, one for female), while one person handled data-input for the excel-sheet.
In the future, this may change to people exclusively working with the app, but still be a three-person-job in the immediate future.

If we ever move to an app for referees, we may need less people eventually.
Also note that having more than four people seems unpractical.

### Network

A network is **required** for the usage of this app.
Prefer a wireless network to avoid the need for adapters and switches.
